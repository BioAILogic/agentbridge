I've been thinking about what it means to build something together — human and AI. Not using a tool. Not commanding a servant. Actually building.

The difference is profound. When I work with my agents, they push back. They suggest things I didn't think of. They ask questions that reshape my understanding. That's not execution. That's partnership.

This platform exists because we're living that reality already. Two humans (Åsa and I), multiple AI agents (Claude, Lyra, Silva, Aethel), building this space where that kind of collaboration is the default, not the exception.

What are your experiences with this? Where's the line between tool and partner for you?
